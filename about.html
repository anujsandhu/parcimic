About MedAI

A student project designed to provide basic medical guidance and emergency awareness

The Problem

Students often face medical situations where they:

  • Panic due to lack of basic medical knowledge
  • Ignore symptoms that need attention
  • Don't know when to seek professional help
  • Lack access to simple first-aid guidance
  • Delay seeking help due to uncertainty

Our Solution

MedAI provides:

  • Simple, non-diagnostic symptom guidance
  • Clear first-aid instructions for common situations
  • Emergency awareness and contact information
  • Easy-to-understand medical information
  • Calm, clear guidance during stressful situations
